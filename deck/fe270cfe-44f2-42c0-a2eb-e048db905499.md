<front>
How do you customize the "overview" section generated for your dbt documentation site? How can you do this for specific packages within your project?
</front>

---

<back>

By supplying a doc block called `__overview__`. You can similarly specify a special `__[package name]__` doc block to overwrite a package's overview.

</back>
