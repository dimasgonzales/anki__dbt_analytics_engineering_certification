<front>

What are the three primary dbt Cloud Release Tracks and what is the purpose of the 'Compatible' track?

</front>

---

<back>

The three tracks are **Latest**, **Compatible**, and **Extended**. 

The **Compatible** track provides stability by receiving updates less frequently than 'Latest', making it the recommended choice for Production environments to prevent unexpected breaking changes.

</back>
