<front>

You are planning a breaking change to the `fct_orders` model. How can you proactively identify which external BI dashboards will be affected?

</front>

---

<back>

You should consult the **dbt Documentation/DAG lineage**. If the dashboards are correctly defined as **exposures** within your dbt project, they will appear as downstream nodes from `fct_orders`, allowing you to assess impact before merging the change.

</back>
