<front>

You need to configure your project so that the database password is never committed to git, but is available during execution. How do you accomplish this?

</front>

---

<back>

Store the password in an environment variable (e.g., `DBT_PASSWORD`) on your local machine or CI system, and reference it in your `profiles.yml`:

```yaml
password: "{{ env_var('DBT_PASSWORD') }}"
```

</back>
