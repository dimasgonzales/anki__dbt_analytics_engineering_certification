<front>

Your production raw data resides in the `RAW_PROD` database, but your QA raw data is in `RAW_QA`. How do you configure `sources.yml` to handle this dynamically without maintaining two files?

</front>

---

<back>

Use Jinja logic within the `database` field of the source definition.

```yaml
sources:
  - name: backend_data
    database: |
      {%- if target.name == 'prod' -%} RAW_PROD
      {%- else -%} RAW_QA
      {%- endif -%}
```

</back>
