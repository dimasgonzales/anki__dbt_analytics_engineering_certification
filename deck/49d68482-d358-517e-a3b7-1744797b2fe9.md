<front>

Your CI pipeline is taking too long because it rebuilds the entire project for every Pull Request. How should you configure the CI job to fix this?

</front>

---

<back>

Enable **deferral** (Slim CI) in the job settings.

By selecting "Defer to a previous run" and configuring the job to compare against the production state, dbt will identify and run only the modified models (and their downstream dependencies) while referencing existing production data for upstream dependencies.

</back>
