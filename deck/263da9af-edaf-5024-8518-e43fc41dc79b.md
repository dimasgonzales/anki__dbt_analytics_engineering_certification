<front>

In the context of dbt project structure, what is the purpose of an 'intermediate model'?

</front>

---

<back>

Intermediate models act as **Intermediate Representations (IR)** that break down complex transformations into manageable steps. They sit between source data and final marts, allowing you to isolate logic, validate data states, and simplify the final downstream models.

</back>
