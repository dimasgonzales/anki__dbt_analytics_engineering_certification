<front>

Which attributes regarding data latency are associated with an exposure's parent sources?

</front>

---

<back>

Exposures can surface freshness data regarding their parent sources, specifically the `maxLoadedAt` timestamp and the configured `warnAfter` and `errorAfter` criteria. This links the freshness of raw data sources directly to the downstream consumption artifact.

</back>
