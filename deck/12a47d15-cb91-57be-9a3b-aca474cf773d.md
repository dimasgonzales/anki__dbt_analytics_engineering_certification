<front>

What two specific JSON artifacts are generated by the `dbt docs generate` command, and what is the primary data source for each?

</front>

---

<back>

1. `manifest.json`: Derived from parsing your **project code and configuration**.
2. `catalog.json`: Derived from **database introspection** (querying the information schema).

Together, these populate the documentation site.

</back>
