<front>

How does dbt populate the `parentIds` field in the lineage graph?

</front>

---

<back>

dbt populates `parentIds` by parsing the **`ref()` function** calls within a model's SQL.

When you use `ref('upstream_model')`, dbt explicitly links the current model to the upstream model's unique ID, establishing a dependency edge in the DAG.

</back>
