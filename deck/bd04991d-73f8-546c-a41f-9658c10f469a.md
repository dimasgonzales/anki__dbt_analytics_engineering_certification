<front>

What is the primary use case for dbt seeds, and what type of data should generally be avoided?

</front>

---

<back>

Seeds are designed for static, infrequently changing data that benefits from version control (e.g., country codes, internal mapping lists). 

They should **not** be used for large raw data exports or sensitive PII, as they are not optimized for performance or security like standard ETL/ELT processes.

</back>
