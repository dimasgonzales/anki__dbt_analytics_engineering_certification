<front>

In dbt node selection syntax, what is the difference between placing the plus operator (`+`) before a model name versus after it?

</front>

---

<back>

Placing the plus operator before the model name (`+model`) selects the model and all its **upstream parents**. Placing it after the model name (`model+`) selects the model and all its **downstream children**.

</back>
