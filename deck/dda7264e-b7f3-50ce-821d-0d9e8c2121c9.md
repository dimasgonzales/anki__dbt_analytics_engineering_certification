<front>

What is the purpose of adding a minus sign (`-`) inside Jinja delimiters, such as `{%- ... -%}` or `{{- ... -}}`?

</front>

---

<back>

The minus sign indicates **whitespace control**. It strips newlines and whitespace from the side of the block where the minus sign is placed, resulting in cleaner, more readable compiled SQL.

</back>
