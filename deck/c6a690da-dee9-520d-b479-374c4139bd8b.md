<front>

What is a critical limitation of `dependencies.yml` compared to `packages.yml` regarding configuration syntax?

</front>

---

<back>

`dependencies.yml` does **not** support Jinja rendering. It is strictly for static configuration. If you need dynamic values, such as `{{ env_var(...) }}`, you must use `packages.yml`.

</back>
