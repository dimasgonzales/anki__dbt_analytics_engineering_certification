<front>

Why do window functions (like `row_number()` or `sum() over()`) present a challenge in standard `incremental` models?

</front>

---

<back>

Window functions generally require a full view of historical data to calculate correct rankings or totals. Standard incremental runs filter for only *new* rows, which breaks the calculation context. You must explicitly join to existing data or adjust the lookback window to ensure accuracy.

</back>
