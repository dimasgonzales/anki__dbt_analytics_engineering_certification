<front>

What is the purpose of the `keepalives_idle` configuration in a Materialize `profiles.yml` file?

</front>

---

<back>

It controls the duration of inactivity before sending a keepalive probe to maintain the connection. It should be **lowered** (e.g., to `0`) if users encounter `SSL SYSCALL error: EOF detected` to prevent intermediate network devices from closing idle connections.

</back>
