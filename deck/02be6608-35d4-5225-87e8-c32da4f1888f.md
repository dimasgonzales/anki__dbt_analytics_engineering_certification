<front>

You have meticulously documented your models in `schema.yml`, but your BI team complains that they cannot see the column descriptions in Snowflake/BigQuery. What is the most likely cause?

</front>

---

<back>

The `persist_docs` configuration is likely disabled or missing in `dbt_project.yml`. Without setting this to `true`, dbt generates the documentation website but does not push the metadata to the data warehouse.

</back>
