<front>

What is the order of precedence regarding materialization configurations defined in `dbt_project.yml` versus a model's `config()` block?

</front>

---

<back>

Configurations defined in the in-file `config()` block take precedence over settings in `dbt_project.yml`.

Specific model configs always override project-level or folder-level defaults.

</back>
