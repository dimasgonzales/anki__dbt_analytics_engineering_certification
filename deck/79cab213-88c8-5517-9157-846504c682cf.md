<front>

You need to ensure the combination of `country_code` and `item_id` is unique, but you do not have a surrogate key or external packages. How can you use the built-in unique test for this?

</front>

---

<back>

You can use the built-in `unique` test on one of the columns but override the `column_name` argument with a **SQL expression** that concatenates the fields.

```yaml
columns:
  - name: item_id
    data_tests:
      - unique:
          config:
            column_name: "country_code || '-' || item_id"
```

</back>
