<front>

How do you instruct Jinja to strip whitespace (newlines or spaces) generated by tags in the compiled SQL?

</front>

---

<back>

Include a minus sign (`-`) inside the delimiter adjacent to the curly brace.

- `{%- ... %}` trims whitespace *before* the block.
- `{% ... -%}` trims whitespace *after* the block.

</back>
