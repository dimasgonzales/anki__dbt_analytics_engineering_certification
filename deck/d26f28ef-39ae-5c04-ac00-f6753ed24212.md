<front>

When debugging a slow dbt job, what is the specific purpose of the dbt Cloud 'Model Timing' tab versus the Data Warehouse's 'Query Profile'?

</front>

---

<back>

The **Model Timing** tab identifies *which* specific model is the bottleneck (longest duration). The **Query Profile** (in the warehouse console) explains *why* that specific query is slow by showing execution details like table scans, join inefficiency, or disk spilling.

</back>
