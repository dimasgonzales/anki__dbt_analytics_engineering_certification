<front>

What global Jinja variable allows you to access the string representation of the currently installed dbt version?

</front>

---

<back>

The `dbt_version` variable.

It is available in the global dbt context and is primarily used for debugging, auditing logs, or checking macro compatibility across different dbt versions.

</back>
