<front>

What is the "Alongside" refactoring strategy in the context of dbt migrations?

</front>

---

<back>

It is the practice of creating new dbt models in a separate directory (e.g., `models/marts`) while keeping the original legacy SQL models running in parallel. This allows for development and auditing without disrupting current downstream users.

</back>
