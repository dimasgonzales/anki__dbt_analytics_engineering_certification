<front>

You define `tags: ['hourly']` in `dbt_project.yml` and `tags: ['finance']` in a model's schema.yml file. What is the resulting tag list for the model?

</front>

---

<back>

The resulting tags are `['hourly', 'finance']`.

Tags are an **additive** configuration. Instead of the more specific schema.yml tag replacing the project-level tag, dbt combines both lists.

</back>
