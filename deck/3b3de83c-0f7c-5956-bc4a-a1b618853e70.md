<front>

What is the primary specific use case for choosing a dbt Python model over a standard SQL model?

</front>

---

<back>

Python models are designed for advanced analytics, machine learning (training or inference), and complex statistical analysis that are difficult or impossible to express in SQL. They allow you to leverage data science libraries like pandas, scikit-learn, or BigFrames directly within your pipeline.

</back>
