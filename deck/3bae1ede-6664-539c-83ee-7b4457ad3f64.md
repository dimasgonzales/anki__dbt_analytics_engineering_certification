<front>

What permission set should be assigned to a Service Token used for embedding Data Health Tiles in external tools?

</front>

---

<back>

You should assign the **'Metadata Only'** permission set. This adheres to the principle of least privilege by limiting the token's access strictly to the metadata APIs required to render the tile.

</back>
