<front>

You view your project's dbt documentation site and notice that the "Source Freshness" section is completely empty. What is the likely cause?

</front>

---

<back>

You likely failed to run `dbt source freshness` before generating the docs. The documentation site requires the `sources.json` artifact produced by this command to display freshness status.

</back>
