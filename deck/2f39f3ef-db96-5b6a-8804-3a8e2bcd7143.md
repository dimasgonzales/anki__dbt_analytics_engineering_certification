<front>

How does the `print()` function behave when dbt is executed with the `--quiet` flag?

</front>

---

<back>

Messages generated by `print()` are **not** suppressed by the `--quiet` flag. They will still appear in stdout alongside error logs, even though standard non-error system logs are hidden.

</back>
