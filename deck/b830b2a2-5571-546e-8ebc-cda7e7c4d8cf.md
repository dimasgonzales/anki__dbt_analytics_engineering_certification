<front>

What does the `--quiet` (or `-q`) flag accomplish when running dbt commands?

</front>

---

<back>

It suppresses all non-error logs (INFO, WARN) in the standard output. Only error logs and output specifically generated by the `{{ print() }}` macro are displayed.

</back>
