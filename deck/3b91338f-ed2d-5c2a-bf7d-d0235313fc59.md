<front>

What are the differences between the `append` and `merge` incremental strategies in dbt?

</front>

---

<back>

`append` simply adds new rows to the destination table and is best for logs where data never changes. `merge` checks for existing records using a `unique_key` to update them (upsert) while inserting new records, ensuring the table reflects the latest state.

</back>
