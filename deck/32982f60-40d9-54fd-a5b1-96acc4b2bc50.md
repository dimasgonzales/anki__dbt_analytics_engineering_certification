<front>

What specific debugging capability does the dbt Fusion Engine offer for troubleshooting complex SQL models with multiple transformation steps?

</front>

---

<back>

Fusion offers **Inline CTE Previews**. This feature allows developers to interactively execute and preview the results of specific Common Table Expressions (CTEs) within the editor, isolating logic errors at the step level without executing the entire model.

</back>
