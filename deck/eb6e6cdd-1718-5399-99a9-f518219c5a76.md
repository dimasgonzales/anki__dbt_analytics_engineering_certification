<front>

What is the functional difference between the `{{ }}` and `{% %}` delimiters in Jinja?

</front>

---

<back>

1. `{{ }}` are for **Expressions**: They output text or values (e.g., variables, macro results) to the compiled SQL.
2. `{% %}` are for **Statements**: They handle control flow (loops, logic) or definitions and do *not* output text directly.

</back>
