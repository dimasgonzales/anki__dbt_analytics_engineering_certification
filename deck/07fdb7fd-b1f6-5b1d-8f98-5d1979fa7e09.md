<front>

How does the Materialize dbt adapter map the standard `table` and `incremental` materializations?

</front>

---

<back>

In the Materialize adapter, `table` creates a **materialized view** (not a physical table). The `incremental` materialization is **unsupported** and raises an error, as Materialize views inherently maintain up-to-date results without manual incremental logic.

</back>
