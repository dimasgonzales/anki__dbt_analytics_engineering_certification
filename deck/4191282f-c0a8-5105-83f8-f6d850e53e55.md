<front>

What languages are supported for defining models in dbt Core v1.3+ and dbt Cloud?

</front>

---

<back>

dbt supports **SQL** (`.sql` files) and **Python** (`.py` files).

Regardless of the language chosen, dbt compiles and executes these models as transformations directly within the data warehouse.

</back>
