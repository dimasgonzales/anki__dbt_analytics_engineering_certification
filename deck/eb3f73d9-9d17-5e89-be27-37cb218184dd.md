<front>

What specific information does the `--debug` (or `-d`) flag reveal that is normally hidden?

</front>

---

<back>

It redirects debug-level logs to standard output, showing:
1. Adapter interactions (opening/closing connections)
2. Raw SQL compilation and execution details
3. Full Python stack traces for errors

</back>
