<front>

What are the recommended default materializations for **Staging** models versus **Marts** models in a standard dbt architecture?

</front>

---

<back>

Staging models should default to **views**, and Marts models should default to **tables**.

Staging models are intermediate building blocks generally not queried by end-users, while Marts require high performance for BI tools and analysis.

</back>
