<front>

What metadata properties are available to define the characteristics of an exposure in `exposures.yml`?

</front>

---

<back>

Key metadata properties include:
*   `type`: The category (e.g., `dashboard`, `notebook`, `application`)
*   `url`: A direct link to the external asset
*   `description`: Text describing the asset
*   `owner`: Contact information (name/email) for the stakeholder

</back>
