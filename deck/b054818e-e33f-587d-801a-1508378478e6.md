<front>

How do you manually define the upstream dependencies of an exposure in dbt?

</front>

---

<back>

You must define the `depends_on` property within the exposure's YAML block. This property accepts a list of `ref()` models or `source()` tables to establish the lineage link.

```yaml
exposure:
  - name: my_dashboard
    depends_on:
      - ref('model_name')
      - source('source_name', 'table_name')
```

</back>
