<front>

How does State-Aware Orchestration in dbt Fusion improve upon standard `state:modified` logic regarding downstream dependencies?

</front>

---

<back>

It utilizes **column-level lineage** rather than just file timestamps or hashes. Fusion checks if the specific columns used by downstream models have changed; if a modification is limited to an unused column, Fusion avoids rebuilding the downstream dependencies, saving compute resources.

</back>
