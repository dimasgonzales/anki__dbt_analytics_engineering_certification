<front>

In dbt-athena, what configuration is strictly required to use the `insert_overwrite` incremental strategy?

</front>

---

<back>

You must configure `partitioned_by`. Without partitions, dbt will default to the `append` strategy even if `insert_overwrite` is specified in the config.
```sql
partitioned_by=['date_col']
```

</back>
