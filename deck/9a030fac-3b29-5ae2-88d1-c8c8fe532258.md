<front>

What is the function of the `dbt retry` command?

</front>

---

<back>

It allows you to rerun a job starting specifically from the point of failure.

dbt reads the artifacts from the previous run (like `run_results.json`) to determine which nodes failed or were skipped, ignoring those that already succeeded.

</back>
