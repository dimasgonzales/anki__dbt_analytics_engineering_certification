<front>

What **dbt\_project.yml** field is used to specify the version of `dbt_project.yml` "syntax" that you are using?

</front>

---

<back>

**config-version**. Without specifying `config-version: 2`, dbt will assume that your `dbt_project.yml` uses version 1 syntax, which was deprecated in dbt v0.19.0

</back>
