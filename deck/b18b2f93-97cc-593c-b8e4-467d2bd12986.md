<front>

Your production pipeline runs `dbt run --warn-error`. After upgrading dbt versions, the pipeline fails immediately despite no changes to the model code. What is the most likely cause?

</front>

---

<back>

The dbt upgrade likely introduced a new **deprecation warning**.

Because `--warn-error` is enabled, this otherwise benign warning was promoted to an error, causing the pipeline to fail.

Rather than a blanket `--warn-error` flag, configuring specific warning options offers a more robust and safer practice for production environments.

</back>
