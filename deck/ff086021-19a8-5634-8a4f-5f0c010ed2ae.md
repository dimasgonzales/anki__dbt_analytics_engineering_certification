<front>

Why is it recommended to structure dbt models using Functional CTEs and end with a `select *` from the final CTE?

</front>

---

<back>

This structure significantly improves **debuggability**. By separating transformations into logical steps (Functional CTEs), a developer can audit the data at any stage by simply changing the final `select` statement to target an upstream CTE instead of the final one.

</back>
