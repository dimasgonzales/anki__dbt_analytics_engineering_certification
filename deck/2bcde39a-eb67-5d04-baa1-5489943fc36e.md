<front>

Does the `analyses/` directory support the use of the `{{ ref() }}` function?

</front>

---

<back>

Yes. Even though they are not materialized, analyses support full Jinja functionality, including `{{ ref() }}`, `{{ source() }}`, and macros. This ensures dependencies are resolved correctly based on the current environment configuration.

</back>
