<front>

What are the three primary Jinja delimiters used in dbt and what is the function of each?

</front>

---

<back>

1. **Expressions** `{{ ... }}`: Prints output (strings/variables) to the compiled SQL.
2. **Statements** `{% ... %}`: Executes control flow logic (loops, ifs, sets) without outputting text.
3. **Comments** `{# ... #}`: Prevents execution and does not appear in the compiled file.

</back>
