<front>

You want to prevent your development runs from querying the upstream 'finance' project's Production data. How can you configure dbt to query a safer upstream dataset automatically?

</front>

---

<back>

Ensure the upstream 'finance' project has a designated **Staging** environment configured in dbt Cloud. dbt will automatically prefer resolving references to the Staging environment (if it has a successful run) over Production during downstream development runs.

</back>
