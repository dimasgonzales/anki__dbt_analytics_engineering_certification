<front>

You copy a configuration snippet from a website into your `dbt_project.yml`. Immediately after, every dbt command fails with a parsing error, even though the keys look correct. What is the most likely cause?

</front>

---

<back>

The copied snippet likely contained **tab characters** or invalid Unicode characters. To fix this, replace all tabs with spaces (usually 2 or 4 spaces per indentation level) to comply with valid YAML syntax.

</back>
