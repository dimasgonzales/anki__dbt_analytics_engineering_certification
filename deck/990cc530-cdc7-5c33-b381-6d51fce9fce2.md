<front>

how do you select the **union** of two selectors?

</front>

---

<back>

Separate the selectors with a **space**, e.g. `dbt run --select +snowplow_sessions +fct_orders` will select `snowplow_sessions`, its parents, *and* `fct_orders` and its parents.

</back>
