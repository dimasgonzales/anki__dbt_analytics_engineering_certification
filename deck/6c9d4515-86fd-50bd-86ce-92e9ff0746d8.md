<front>

How does the processing logic of an `incremental` model differ from a `table` materialization during a standard run?

</front>

---

<back>

A `table` materialization drops and rebuilds the entire dataset on every run. An `incremental` model executes logic to only transform and load new or changed data since the last run, usually by filtering against the max timestamp of the destination table.

</back>
