<front>

Your team wants to ensure all public-facing models have documentation. How would you use 'Lenses' to identify gaps in your project?

</front>

---

<back>

You would use a Lens to filter the DAG based on metadata criteria. Specifically, you can filter for models where `access: public` AND `description: null` (or missing), allowing you to visually isolate and remediate the un-governed models in the lineage graph.

</back>
