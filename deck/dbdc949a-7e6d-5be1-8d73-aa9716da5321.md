<front>

What is the purpose of the `--sample` flag when running dbt commands?

</front>

---

<back>

The `--sample` flag generates filtered references and sources based on time-based sampling during execution. It allows developers to validate model logic and outputs without incurring the time and cost associated with building the full dataset.

</back>
