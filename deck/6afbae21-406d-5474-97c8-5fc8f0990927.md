<front>

A developer attempts to run `dbt compile` while their VPN is disconnected and receives a connection error. Why does this happen even though they are not trying to materialize tables?

</front>

---

<back>

Even though `dbt compile` does not write data, it runs 'introspective queries' to understand the state of the database schema. Without an active connection, dbt cannot populate its cache or resolve data-dependent macros, resulting in a failure.

</back>
