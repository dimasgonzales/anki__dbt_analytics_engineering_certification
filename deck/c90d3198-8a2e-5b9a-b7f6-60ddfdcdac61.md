<front>

What is the difference in configuration syntax between **generic tests** and **singular tests**?

</front>

---

<back>

**Generic tests** are configured in YAML files under the `config` key.
**Singular tests** (SQL files) are configured using the `{{ config() }}` Jinja macro at the top of the file.

</back>
