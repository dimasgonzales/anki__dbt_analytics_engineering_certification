<front>

A scheduled daily job is taking significantly longer than expected to complete. How would you use the dbt Cloud UI to identify the specific models causing the delay on the critical path?

</front>

---

<back>

Navigate to a recent successful run of the job and click the **Model Timing** tab. Observe the longest bars in the visualization to identify which models have the highest duration and are blocking downstream dependencies, causing the overall job latency.

</back>
