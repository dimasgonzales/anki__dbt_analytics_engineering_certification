<front>

What are the quantity limits for Development, Production, and General environments within a single dbt Cloud project?

</front>

---

<back>

A dbt Cloud project permits exactly **one** Development environment and **one** Production environment. While you are limited to one Staging environment, you may create an **unlimited** number of General deployment environments.

</back>
