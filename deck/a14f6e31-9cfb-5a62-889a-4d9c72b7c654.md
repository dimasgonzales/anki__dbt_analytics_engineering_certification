<front>

Which materialization type cannot be combined with `access: public` configuration?

</front>

---

<back>

`ephemeral` models cannot be configured as `public`.

Because ephemeral models are interpolated as CTEs into downstream queries and do not exist as distinct database objects, they cannot serve as stable public interfaces for cross-project references.

</back>
