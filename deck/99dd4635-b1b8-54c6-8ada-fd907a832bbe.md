<front>

You need to ensure that the combination of `user_id` and `session_id` is unique across the `sessions` model. How should this be configured in YAML?

</front>

---

<back>

Apply a **model-level** test, as the logic involves multiple columns.

```yaml
models:
  - name: sessions
    data_tests:
      - dbt_utils.unique_combination_of_columns:
          combination_of_columns:
            - user_id
            - session_id
```

</back>
