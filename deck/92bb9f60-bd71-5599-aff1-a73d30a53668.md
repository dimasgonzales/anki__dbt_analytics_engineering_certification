<front>

What is a dbt 'exposure' and where is it defined?

</front>

---

<back>

An exposure is a resource that defines a downstream consumer of your dbt project, such as a dashboard or application, extending the DAG lineage beyond the warehouse. Exposures are defined in `.yml` files using the top-level `exposures:` key.

</back>
