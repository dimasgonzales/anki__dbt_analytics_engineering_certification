<front>

In dbt Core v1.9+, what three specific properties are available within the `model.batch` object during a microbatch execution?

</front>

---

<back>

1. `model.batch.id` (The unique identifier for the batch)
2. `model.batch.event_time_start` (The inclusive start timestamp)
3. `model.batch.event_time_end` (The exclusive end timestamp)

</back>
