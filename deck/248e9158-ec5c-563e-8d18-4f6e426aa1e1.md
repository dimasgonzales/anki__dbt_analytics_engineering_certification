<front>

According to dbt best practices, what are the four components of a Standardized CTE structure?

</front>

---

<back>

1. **Import CTEs**: Simple selects from sources or refs.
2. **Logical CTEs**: Where transformations and logic happen.
3. **Final CTE**: Joins the logical components.
4. **Final Select**: A simple `select * from final`.

</back>
