<front>

How can you set custom attributes on your resources that will be viewable in auto-generated documentation?

</front>

---

<back>

Using the `meta` config. This config takes a dictionary of arbitrary keys that you decide upon, and these attributes are propagated to the project's manifest.json and auto-generated docs.

</back>
