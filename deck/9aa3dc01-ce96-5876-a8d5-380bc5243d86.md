<front>

What is the primary purpose of the `dbt-labs/audit_helper` package during a migration project?

</front>

---

<back>

It provides macros to programmatically compare the data outputs of two relations (e.g., a legacy table vs. a new dbt model). It helps validate logic parity by identifying row-by-row differences.

</back>
