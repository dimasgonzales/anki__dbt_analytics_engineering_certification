<front>

In dbt Cloud, where can you find the detailed `stdout` and `stderr` logs for specific execution steps, such as `dbt run` or `git clone`?

</front>

---

<back>

In the **Run Summary** tab of a job run. You can expand individual steps (e.g., the failed `dbt build` step) to view the raw console logs, which contain specific SQL errors or Jinja compilation issues necessary for troubleshooting.

</back>
