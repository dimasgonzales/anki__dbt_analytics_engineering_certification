<front>

Beyond file-level dependencies defined by `ref()`, what two types of granular lineage does dbt Explorer visualize?

</front>

---

<back>

1. **Column-Level Lineage**: Traces specific fields/columns from the raw source through transformations to the final mart.
2. **Cross-Project Lineage**: Maps dependencies between different projects (enabled by dbt Mesh).

</back>
