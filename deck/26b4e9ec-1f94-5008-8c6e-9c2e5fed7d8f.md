<front>

Why should models with complex join logic exposed to BI users generally use 'table' or 'incremental' materialization instead of the default 'view'?

</front>

---

<back>

Views execute the underlying SQL query—including expensive joins—every time the model is queried by the end user. Materializing as a **table** or **incremental** model runs the logic once during the build process, storing the results for faster retrieval by downstream tools.

</back>
