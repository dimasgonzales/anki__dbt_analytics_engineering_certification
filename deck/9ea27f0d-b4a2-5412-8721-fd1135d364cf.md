<front>

What is the purpose of the `incremental_predicates` configuration in dbt incremental models?

</front>

---

<back>

It optimizes performance by pushing custom filters down to the merge operation, limiting the scan to relevant partitions or rows in the destination table. This prevents expensive full table scans when updating large tables.

</back>
