<front>

What feature in dbt Explorer automates the discovery of missing tests and documentation, similar to the `dbt_project_evaluator` package?

</front>

---

<back>

The **Project Recommendations** (or Health Checks) feature. It scans the project metadata to flag best practice violations, such as models missing unique tests on primary keys, missing descriptions, or poor structural patterns.

</back>
