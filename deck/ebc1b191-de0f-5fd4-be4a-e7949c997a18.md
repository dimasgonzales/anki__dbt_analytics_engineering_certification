<front>

How do exposures relate to **Data Freshness** in a deployment context?

</front>

---

<back>

Exposures allow for **integrated orchestration**. By defining the relationship between dbt models and downstream tools, orchestration platforms (like dbt Cloud) can automatically trigger a refresh of the downstream asset (e.g., a Tableau extract) immediately after the dbt job succeeds.

</back>
