<front>

How does the `--defer` flag impact the compilation of ephemeral models?

</front>

---

<back>

Ephemeral models are **never deferred**.

Because ephemeral models are not materialized in the database, they cannot be referenced from a production schema. They are always compiled as CTEs and passed through into the downstream SQL, regardless of deferral settings.

</back>
