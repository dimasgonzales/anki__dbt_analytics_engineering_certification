<front>

What are the two specific environment types available in dbt Cloud and what is the primary use case for each?

</front>

---

<back>

The two types are **Development** and **Deployment**.
*   **Development:** Used by the IDE and CLI for interactive coding and testing by developers.
*   **Deployment:** Used for scheduled jobs and production runs to update live data.

</back>
