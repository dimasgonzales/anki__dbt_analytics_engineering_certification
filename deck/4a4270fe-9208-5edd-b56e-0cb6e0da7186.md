<front>

What is the primary purpose of the `groups` configuration key in dbt?

</front>

---

<back>

It defines collections of models to assign **ownership** (e.g., team emails) and establish **access control contracts** (public vs. private models) for cross-project collaboration (dbt Mesh).

</back>
