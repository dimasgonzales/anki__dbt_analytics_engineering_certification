<front>

You notice a Job log reports "Models built: 5, Models reused: 20". What mechanism is dbt using, and why is this beneficial?

</front>

---

<back>

dbt is using **State-Aware Orchestration** (often linked to Deferral/Slim CI). It indicates that 20 upstream models were unchanged and were not rebuilt, saving significant warehouse compute costs and reducing job runtime.

</back>
