<front>

What is the fundamental difference between a 'Property' and a 'Configuration' in dbt?

</front>

---

<back>

**Properties** describe a resource (metadata, descriptions, tests) and are strictly defined in `.yml` files.

**Configurations** instruct dbt on how to build the resource (materialization, schema, tags) and can be defined in `.sql` files (via macros), `dbt_project.yml`, or `.yml` property files.

</back>
