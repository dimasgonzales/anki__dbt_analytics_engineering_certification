<front>

how do you select the **intersection** of two selectors?

</front>

---

<back>

Separate the selectors with a **comma**, e.g. `dbt run --select +snowplow_sessions,+fct_orders` will select common ancestors of *both* `snowplow_sessions` *and* `fct_orders`

</back>
