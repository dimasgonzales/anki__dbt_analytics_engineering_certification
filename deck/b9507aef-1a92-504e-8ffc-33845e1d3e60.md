<front>

What **dbt\_project.yml** field is used to specify what profile your dbt project should use to connect to your data warehouse? When is this option required?

</front>

---

<back>

**profile**. This configuration is optional when developing in dbt Cloud, but required when developing locally (unless you pass the `--profile` flag through the CLI instead)

</back>
