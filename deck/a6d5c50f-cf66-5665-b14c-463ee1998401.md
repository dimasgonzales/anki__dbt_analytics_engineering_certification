<front>

What is the primary purpose of a dbt **exposure**?

</front>

---

<back>

Exposures define and document downstream consumers of your dbt project, such as dashboards, ML models, or reverse ETL syncs. They allow you to extend the project's lineage (DAG) beyond the data warehouse tables to the final consumption layer.

</back>
