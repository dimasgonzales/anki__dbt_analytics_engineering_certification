<front>

Production jobs are running successfully, but a new developer is receiving connection errors in the dbt Cloud IDE. What is the most likely cause?

</front>

---

<back>

The developer likely has not configured their personal credentials in their **Profile Settings**. While the Environment has valid credentials for jobs, the IDE requires unique credentials for every developer to ensure development activity is isolated (e.g., separate schemas).

</back>
