<front>

What is the primary purpose of the dbt Cloud Audit Log regarding job stability?

</front>

---

<back>

The Audit Log is the primary tool to detect **configuration drift**. It tracks specific changes to job definitions, environments, and connections, allowing administrators to identify "who changed what and when" if a job fails without recent code changes.

</back>
