<front>

What are the environment type and dbt version requirements for successfully deploying metrics to the dbt Semantic Layer?

</front>

---

<back>

1. **Environment Type:** Must be a 'Deployment' environment (metrics cannot be served from Development environments).
2. **dbt Version:** Must be version 1.6 or higher.

</back>
