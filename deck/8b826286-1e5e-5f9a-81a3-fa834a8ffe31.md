<front>

What is the function of the hyphen (`-`) in Jinja tags like `{%-` or `-%}`?

</front>

---

<back>

It performs whitespace control.

Adding a hyphen to the start of a tag (`{%-`) strips whitespace preceding the block, and adding it to the end (`-%}`) strips whitespace following the block. This helps prevent excessive newlines in the compiled SQL.

</back>
