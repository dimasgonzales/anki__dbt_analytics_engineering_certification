<front>

In dbt model versioning, how does dbt classify a version that has an identifier greater than the configured `latest_version`?

</front>

---

<back>

It is classified as a **prerelease** version.

Versions higher than `latest` are considered 'prerelease' (typically for testing), while versions lower than `latest` are considered 'old'.

</back>
