<front>

In dbt Cloud, where are database credentials defined for Development environments versus Deployment environments?

</front>

---

<back>

**Deployment** environments use the connection details defined at the Environment level (often service accounts). **Development** environments require individual developers to set personal credentials in their specific **Profile Settings**.

</back>
