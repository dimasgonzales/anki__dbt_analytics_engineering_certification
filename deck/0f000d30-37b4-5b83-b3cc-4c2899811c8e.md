<front>

What configuration options are available in dbt to prevent parser errors on valid but complex SQL, such as User-Defined Functions (UDFs)?

</front>

---

<back>

You can configure `static_analysis` to `off` or `unsafe`. This skips the validation step that might incorrectly flag complex syntax as errors.

```yaml
+static_analysis: off
```

</back>
