<front>

By default, what is the naming convention for the database schema where dbt stores test failure records?

</front>

---

<back>

dbt appends `_dbt_test__audit` to your target schema. 

For example:
If your target schema is `dbt_jdoe`, failures are stored in `dbt_jdoe_dbt_test__audit`.

</back>
