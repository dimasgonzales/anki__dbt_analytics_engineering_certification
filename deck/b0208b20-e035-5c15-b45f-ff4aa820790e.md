<front>

How does dbt determine the `latest_version` of a model if the `latest_version` property is omitted from the configuration?

</front>

---

<back>

dbt defaults to the version with the **largest identifier**.

dbt compares the defined versions (numerically or alphabetically) and automatically treats the highest one as the `latest_version` for unpinned references.

</back>
