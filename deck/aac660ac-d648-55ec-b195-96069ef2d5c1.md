<front>

What specific visualization in dbt Cloud allows you to analyze model duration and execution order to identify bottlenecks in a job?

</front>

---

<back>

The **Model Timing** tab. It displays a Gantt-style view of the job's execution, specifically highlighting the top 1% of longest-running models. This allows engineers to visually identify slow steps and opportunities for parallelization in the DAG.

</back>
