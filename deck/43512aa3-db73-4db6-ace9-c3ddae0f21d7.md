<front>
How can you apply a generic test to only a subset of data?
</front>

---

<back>

By invoking the `where` attribute of the test's config block. For example:  
  
  - unique:  
      config:  
        where: "order\_date > '2018-03-01'"  
  
Will apply a unique test to just those records with an order date after 2018-03-01

</back>
