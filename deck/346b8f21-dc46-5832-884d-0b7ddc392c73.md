<front>

A Lead Developer needs to debug a permissions error and attempts to view 'Account settings > Audit log', but the menu option is missing. What is the likely reason?

</front>

---

<back>

The developer lacks the required **Account Admin** or **Account Viewer** role. Even with high-level project permissions, they cannot view account-level audit logs and must escalate the request to an administrator.

</back>
