<front>

If an upstream model uses introspection, how does this impact the static analysis timing for its downstream descendants?

</front>

---

<back>

The descendants are automatically opted into 'unsafe' JIT static analysis. Their validation is deferred until runtime (just before execution) rather than happening during the initial project parse (AOT), preventing early optimization for that DAG branch.

</back>
