<front>

What is the hierarchy of precedence for dbt global configurations (flags), from highest to lowest priority?

</front>

---

<back>

1. **CLI options** (Highest)
2. **Environment Variables**
3. **`dbt_project.yml` configuration**
4. **Internal defaults** (Lowest)

The most specific setting (CLI) always overrides general project settings.

</back>
