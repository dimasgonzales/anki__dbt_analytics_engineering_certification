<front>

Given the following setup, what happens when the test for `my_model` fails?

`dbt_project.yml`: `+severity: warn`
`models/schema.yml`: `severity: error`

</front>

---

<back>

The test returns an **Error**.

Specific configurations in the model YAML (`schema.yml`) override general project-level configurations in `dbt_project.yml`.

</back>
