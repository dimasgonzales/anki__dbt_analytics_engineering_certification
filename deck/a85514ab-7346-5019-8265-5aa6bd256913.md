<front>

You have defined a default `owner` for all resources in your `dbt_project.yml`, but you notice your new exposure does not inherit this owner. What is the cause?

</front>

---

<back>

Exposures do not inherit configuration properties (like `owner`) from `dbt_project.yml` or `config()` blocks. Unlike models, exposure metadata must be explicitly defined within the exposure's specific YAML block.

</back>
