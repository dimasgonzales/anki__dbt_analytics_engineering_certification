You are setting up an automatic exposure integration with Tableau Server, but dbt Cloud cannot connect to fetch metadata. What is the most likely cause?

</front>

---

<back>

The dbt Cloud traffic is likely being blocked by a firewall. To troubleshoot, verify that the dbt Cloud IP addresses for your region have been added to the Tableau Server's IP allowlist.
