When analyzing execution metadata to optimize dbt project performance, which specific metrics are most critical to monitor?

</front>

---

<back>

You should primarily monitor 'Longest model executions' to find slow queries and 'Most executed models' to identify frequently built dependencies. High 'Model Test Failure Rates' should also be reviewed as they impact overall pipeline reliability.
