<front>

You are refactoring a core financial model that powers the CFO's dashboard. How does dbt prevent your work-in-progress changes from breaking the live dashboard?

</front>

---

<back>

dbt uses environment isolation to direct your local runs to a separate development schema (e.g., `dbt_yourname`). The CFO's dashboard queries the `production` schema, so your changes remain isolated until they are reviewed, merged, and deployed to production.

</back>
