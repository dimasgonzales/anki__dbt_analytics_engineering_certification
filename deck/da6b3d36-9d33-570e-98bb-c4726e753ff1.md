<front>

Your model fails with a database error: "Unexpected identifier near ','". Your Jinja loop looks correct in the source file. How do you troubleshoot this?

</front>

---

<back>

Open the corresponding file in the `target/compiled/` directory.
This allows you to see the **rendered SQL** exactly as dbt generated it. You will likely find a trailing comma generated by the loop that wasn't visible in the source Jinja code.

</back>
