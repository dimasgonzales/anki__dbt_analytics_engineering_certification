<front>
What **dbt\_project.yml** field is used to specify a custom list of directories where snapshots are located? What is the default?
</front>

---

<back>

**snapshot-paths**. Default is `snapshots`. Unlike with seeds, you cannot co-locate models and snapshots.

</back>
