<front>

Why is it critical to check if a context-dependent object (like `model.batch`) exists before accessing its properties?

</front>

---

<back>

Because these objects may be `None` during specific phases (like parsing) or execution modes (like a full refresh). Accessing properties on `None` will cause the compilation to fail with a runtime error.

</back>
