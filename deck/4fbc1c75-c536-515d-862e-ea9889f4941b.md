<front>

You have a complex loop generating SQL columns, but the compiled code in `target/compiled/` contains hundreds of empty lines, making it hard to debug. How do you clean this up?

</front>

---

<back>

Apply whitespace control characters to your Jinja tags.

Change your loop tags from standard `{% ... %}` to `{%- ... -%}`. This tells the compiler to strip the newlines and spaces usually generated by the logic blocks.

</back>
