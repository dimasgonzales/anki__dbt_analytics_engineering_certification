<front>

In the hierarchy of dbt materializations, when should you use `ephemeral`?

</front>

---

<back>

Use `ephemeral` for lightweight intermediate transformations that are not reused heavily. These models are interpolated as CTEs into downstream models and do not exist as physical objects in the database, helping to keep the warehouse clean.

</back>
