<front>

A team member notices that the dbt Cloud Documentation (Catalog) is not updating despite successful job runs. What configuration setting should you check first?

</front>

---

<back>

Verify that the specific Deployment Environment is marked as the 'Production' environment in the Settings. Only the environment designated as 'Production' generates the canonical catalog for the project.

</back>
