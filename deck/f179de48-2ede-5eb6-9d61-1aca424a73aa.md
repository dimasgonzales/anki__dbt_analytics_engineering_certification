<front>

You need to snapshot a source table that does not contain a reliable `updated_at` timestamp column. Which strategy should you use and why?

</front>

---

<back>

Use the `check` strategy. This strategy compares the values of specific columns (defined in `check_cols`) between the source and the current snapshot to detect changes, whereas the `timestamp` strategy relies entirely on a reliable updated-at column.

</back>
