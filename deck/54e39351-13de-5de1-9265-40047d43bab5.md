What is the 'Alongside' refactoring strategy?

</front>

---

<back>

The 'Alongside' strategy involves creating new dbt models in a separate directory (e.g., `models/marts`) while keeping the legacy SQL processes running.

This allows for side-by-side auditing and ensures the new models are correct before deprecating the old ones.

</back>
