<front>

What is the function of the `threads` setting in `profiles.yml`?

</front>

---

<back>

It determines the maximum number of models dbt will build concurrently (in parallel). It should be tuned based on the warehouse's available compute resources to balance throughput and avoid resource contention.

</back>
