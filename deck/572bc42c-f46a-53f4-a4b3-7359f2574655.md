<front>

Dashboard users complain about "Table not found" errors occurring while your daily `table` materialization models are running. How can you resolve this in dbt?

</front>

---

<back>

Add `ha=true` to the model configuration. This prevents the standard drop-create behavior that creates the availability gap.
```sql
{{ config(materialized='table', ha=true) }}
```

</back>
