<front>

You attempt to execute `dbt run` in one terminal window while `dbt build` is already running in another window for the same project. What will occur?

</front>

---

<back>

The second command will be **blocked** or fail to acquire the lock.

Both `dbt run` and `dbt build` are classified as **Write** commands. dbt enforces a lock to prevent simultaneous Write operations that could corrupt the project state.

</back>
