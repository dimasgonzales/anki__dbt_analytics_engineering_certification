<front>

What is the role of `dbt clone` in a Blue/Green deployment strategy?

</front>

---

<back>

`dbt clone` acts as the **swap mechanism**. It allows you to build and validate the entire project in a staging environment (Green) and then instantly 'promote' it to production (Blue) by cloning the validated metadata, ensuring atomic updates with minimal downtime.

</back>
