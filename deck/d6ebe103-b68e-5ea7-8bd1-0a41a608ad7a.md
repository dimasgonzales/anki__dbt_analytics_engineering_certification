<front>

What is the recommended default materialization for models inside a distributed dbt package?

</front>

---

<back>

`view`.

Views are the lightest weight materialization. This gives the end-user the flexibility to override specific heavy-usage models to `table` or `incremental` in their own project configuration based on their performance requirements.

</back>
