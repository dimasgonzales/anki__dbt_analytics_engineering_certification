<front>

You need to create distinct CI/CD pipelines for three different feature teams to test ad-hoc jobs simultaneously. Which environment type should you use and why?

</front>

---

<back>

You should use **General** environments. Since dbt Cloud allows for unlimited General environments, they are the correct choice for ad-hoc testing or specific feature team needs, whereas Staging, Development, and Production are limited to one per project.

</back>
