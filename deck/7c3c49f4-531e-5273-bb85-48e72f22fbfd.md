<front>

In the Full YAML selector syntax, which keys correspond to the CLI graph operators `+` (children), `+` (parents), and `@` (parents of children)?

</front>

---

<back>

- `+` (children) maps to `children: true`
- `+` (parents) maps to `parents: true`
- `@` (parents of children) maps to `childrens_parents: true`

</back>
