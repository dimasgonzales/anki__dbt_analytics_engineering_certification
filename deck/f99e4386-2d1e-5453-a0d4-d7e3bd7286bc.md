<front>

Which SQL materialization types support dbt model contracts, and which common type does NOT support them?

</front>

---

<back>

Contracts are supported for `table`, `view`, and `incremental` materializations. They are **not** supported for `ephemeral` models, nor are they supported for sources, seeds, or snapshots.

</back>
