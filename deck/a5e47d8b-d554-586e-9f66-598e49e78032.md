<front>

What configuration must be enabled in a dbt Cloud Job to ensure Data Health Tiles display 'Staleness' information?

</front>

---

<back>

The **'Generate source freshness'** toggle must be enabled in the job settings. This ensures the job runs the `dbt source freshness` command before executing the run, capturing the necessary metadata for the tile.

</back>
