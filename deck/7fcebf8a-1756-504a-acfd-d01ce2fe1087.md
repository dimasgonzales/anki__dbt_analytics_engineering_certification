<front>

What specific action is required in dbt Cloud to override state-aware optimization and force a full rebuild of all models?

</front>

---

<back>

You must click the **Clear cache** button in the Environment settings. This wipes the Redis state, ensuring the immediate next run builds everything from scratch (0% reuse).

</back>
