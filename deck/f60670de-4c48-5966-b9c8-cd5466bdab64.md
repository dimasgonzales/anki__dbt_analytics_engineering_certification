What is the 'Import CTE' pattern in dbt modeling?

</front>

---

<back>

The 'Import CTE' pattern involves defining all model dependencies (using `ref` or `source`) in Common Table Expressions (CTEs) at the very top of the SQL file. This creates a clear list of dependencies and allows for early renaming to user-friendly names.

</back>
