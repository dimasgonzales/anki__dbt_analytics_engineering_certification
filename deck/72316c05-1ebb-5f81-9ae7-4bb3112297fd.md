<front>

Your organization requires all SCD Type 2 history tables to use `start_date` and `end_date` instead of dbt's defaults. How would you apply this globally to all snapshots?

</front>

---

<back>

Define the `snapshot_meta_column_names` configuration under the `snapshots:` key in your `dbt_project.yml` file.

```yaml
snapshots:
  config:
    snapshot_meta_column_names:
      dbt_valid_from: start_date
      dbt_valid_to: end_date
```

</back>
