<front>

How do you configure a dbt model to allow it to be referenced by external projects in a dbt Mesh or multi-project architecture?

</front>

---

<back>

You must explicitly set the `access` property to `public` in the model's configuration. By default, models are considered `protected` (internal to the project) and cannot be referenced by other projects.

</back>
