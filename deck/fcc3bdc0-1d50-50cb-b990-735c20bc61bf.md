<front>

How does dbt handle Data Definition Language (DDL) when running a model?

</front>

---

<back>

dbt abstracts DDL away from the developer. You write a `SELECT` statement, and dbt wraps it in the appropriate DDL (e.g., `CREATE VIEW`, `CREATE TABLE`, `INSERT INTO`) based on the configured materialization strategy.

</back>
