<front>

What are the three primary 'Release Tracks' in dbt Cloud and what does 'Extended' provide?

</front>

---

<back>

The tracks are **Latest**, **Compatible**, and **Extended**. The 'Extended' track provides long-term support and stability, ensuring critical production pipelines are protected from breaking changes or immediate deprecations found in newer versions.

</back>
