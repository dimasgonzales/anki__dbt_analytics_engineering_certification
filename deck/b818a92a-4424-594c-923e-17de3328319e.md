<front>

What are three examples of universal `target` attributes available across all dbt adapters?

</front>

---

<back>

Universal attributes available regardless of the database adapter include:
1. `target.name` (e.g., 'dev', 'prod')
2. `target.schema` (the default schema)
3. `target.type` (the adapter type, e.g., 'snowflake', 'bigquery')
4. `target.threads` (configured thread count)

</back>
