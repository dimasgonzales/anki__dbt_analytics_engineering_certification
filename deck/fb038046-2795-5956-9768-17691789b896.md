<front>

Your team notices that the dbt project startup time has increased significantly. How would you scientifically identify the bottleneck?

</front>

---

<back>

Run `dbt parse` and inspect the resulting `target/perf_info.json` file. 

By reviewing keys like `load_all_elapsed` and `parsers` within this file, you can identify exactly which part of the project loading process is causing the delay.

</back>
