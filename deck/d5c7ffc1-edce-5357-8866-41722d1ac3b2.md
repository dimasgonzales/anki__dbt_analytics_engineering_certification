<front>

You are troubleshooting a dbt command but cannot remember the exact flag to ignore failures. How can you find this information without opening a web browser?

</front>

---

<back>

Execute the command with the `--help` flag in your CLI. 

This will print the full list of valid flags and their descriptions to your terminal, allowing you to identify the correct flag (e.g., `--warn-error` or similar depending on the specific command context).

</back>
