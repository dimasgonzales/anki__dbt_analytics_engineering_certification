Your nightly dbt job has slowed down significantly. How would you use the Discovery API to pinpoint the specific models causing the delay?

</front>

---

<back>

To identify bottlenecks, query the `models` node and request the `executionInfo` object. Sort the retrieved models by their `executionTime` in descending order to pinpoint the specific materializations causing delays.
