<front>

What is the primary purpose of dbt Snapshots?

</front>

---

<back>

Snapshots implement **Slowly Changing Dimensions (SCD) Type 2** logic. They record changes in mutable source data over time by detecting modified rows and storing the historical state with valid_from and valid_to timestamps.

</back>
