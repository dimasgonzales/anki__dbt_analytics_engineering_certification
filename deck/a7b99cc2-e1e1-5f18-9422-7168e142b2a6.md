<front>

What is the function of the environment variable `DBT_ACTIVE_EXPOSURES_BUILD_AFTER`?

</front>

---

<back>

It sets a minimum time interval (in minutes) between downstream exposure refreshes. If a dbt job runs before this time limit expires, the exposure refresh step is skipped to prevent redundant updates.

</back>
