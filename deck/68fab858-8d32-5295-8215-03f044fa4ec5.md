<front>

You are analyzing warehouse costs and see expensive queries from the dbt user, but cannot identify which specific dbt model triggered them. What configuration helps resolve this?

</front>

---

<back>

Configure `query-comment` in `dbt_project.yml` to include metadata such as the node name or user.

```yaml
query-comment:
  comment: 'Model: {{ node.name }}'
```

</back>
