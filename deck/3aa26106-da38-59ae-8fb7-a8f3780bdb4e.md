You are building a custom dashboard using dbt artifacts. You need to display the `description` of a source alongside its current freshness status. How would you join the data programmatically?

</front>

---

<back>

Iterate through the results in `sources.json`, grab the `unique_id`, and use it to look up the corresponding `source` node in the `manifest.json` file, where the `description` is stored.

</back>
