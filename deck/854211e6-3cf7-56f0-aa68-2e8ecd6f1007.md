<front>

You receive a parsing error stating that `semantic_models` is an invalid key in your `dbt_project.yml`. What is the likely cause and solution?

</front>

---

<back>

The error is caused by using an underscore (`_`) instead of a hyphen (`-`). In `dbt_project.yml`, top-level keys must use kebab-case.

**Solution:** Change `semantic_models:` to `semantic-models:`.

</back>
