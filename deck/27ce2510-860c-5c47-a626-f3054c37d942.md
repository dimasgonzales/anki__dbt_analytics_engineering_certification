<front>

What is the primary function of the `{{ debug() }}` macro within a dbt project?

</front>

---

<back>

It acts as a breakpoint in your Jinja code. When dbt encounters it, it pauses compilation or execution and opens an interactive **ipdb** (iPython debugger) session, allowing you to inspect variables and the current context.

</back>
