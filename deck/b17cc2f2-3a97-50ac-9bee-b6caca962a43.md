<front>

Which specific dbt Cloud RBAC roles are required to access the Audit Log?

</front>

---

<back>

Access to the Audit Log is strictly limited to users with **Account Admin** or **Account Viewer** licenses. Standard Developers or Read-Only users cannot access this feature.

</back>
